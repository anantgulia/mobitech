MobiTech

MobiTech Teleservices intends to be a market leader in the Telecom industry by providing our clients the most flexible, dynamic, and competitive service solutions available today.

Our Services

  • Basic services including liaisoning, legal bodies permission, etc with BSNL & SEB department
  • Resources Supply (Manpower)
  • In-Building solutions for mobile communication
  • Site Acquisition and Permission(Local Bodies, EB, SCFA and Pollution Board)
  • Site civil Construction
  • GSM/MW mount installation.
  • Tower repairing & tighten.
  • Electrical work to install PP, BB, DCDB & all kind of cable laying etc.
  • BTS and Micro BTS Installation
  • MW and GSM/CDMA Antenna Installation
  • Installation of Additional Carrier
  • BTS Expansion
  • Transmission up-gradation & conversion of PDH to SDH
  • Installation & commissioning of 2/3/4G E-Nodes for LTE technology
  • New Transmission ring installation & commissioning to carry heavy traffic.
  • Also providing team of riggers & electrician to execute activity regarding active networks.
  • Antenna/RRU height increase/decrease as per customer requirement.
